Affogato

Description

Preparation of Ingredients

Create a simple and elegant Italian treat with an affogato. All you need are 1-2 shots of freshly brewed espresso and a scoop of velvety vanilla ice cream. Ensure the espresso is hot and robust to complement the cold sweetness of the ice cream.

Assembly and Presentation

Begin by brewing 1-2 shots of espresso, capturing the rich and aromatic essence. Scoop a generous serving of vanilla ice cream into a glass or cup, creating a delightful base for the affogato. With a flourish, pour the hot espresso over the cold ice cream, allowing the two elements to harmonize.

Serving and Enjoying

The result is a captivating contrast of temperatures and flavors as the hot espresso melds with the cool, creamy ice cream. Serve the affogato immediately, and savor the indulgent experience of this simple yet sophisticated Italian delight. Perfect for a quick pick-me-up or a delightful dessert after a meal, the affogato offers a harmonious blend of coffee and ice cream.

Ingredients

  • 1-2 shots of espresso
  • Vanilla ice cream

Steps

  1. Brew 1-2 shots of espresso.
  2. Scoop a generous serving of vanilla ice cream into a serving glass or cup.
  3. Pour the hot espresso over the ice cream.
  4. Allow the espresso to melt the ice cream slightly
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy the delightful contrast of hot espresso and cold, creamy ice cream.
