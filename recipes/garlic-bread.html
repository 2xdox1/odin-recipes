Italian Garlic Bread

Description

Preparation of Ingredients

For a delightful serving of garlic bread, assemble the essentials: a baguette or Italian bread, butter, minced garlic, fresh parsley, and grated Parmesan cheese. Ensure the butter is softened for easy spreading, and preheat the oven for baking.

Assembly and Baking

Slice the bread horizontally, and generously spread the softened butter and minced garlic mixture on each half. Sprinkle with grated Parmesan and finely chopped fresh parsley. Reassemble the halves, wrap the loaf in foil, and bake until the bread is warm and the flavors meld together.

Serving and Enjoying

Once baked, unwrap the garlic bread and slice it into individual portions. Serve immediately, and revel in the warm, aromatic delight of buttery garlic-infused bread. This simple recipe adds a flavorful side to complement various dishes or stands as a delicious snack on its own.

Ingredients

  • Baguette or Italian bread
  • Butter (softened)
  • Minced garlic
  • Fresh parsley
  • Grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven.
  2. Slice the bread horizontally.
  3. Spread softened butter and minced garlic on each half.
  4. Sprinkle with grated Parmesan and fresh parsley.
  5. Reassemble halves, wrap in foil.
  6. Bake until warm and flavors meld.
  7. Unwrap, slice, and serve immediately. Enjoy!
